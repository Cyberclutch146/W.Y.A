# 🚀 IEMxRCC: Campus Recommendation Engine Roadmap

This document outlines 30 advanced features to transform the IEMxRCC recommendation system into a fully-aware, high-engagement "Campus Brain."

---

## 🏗️ Phase 1: The Core 5 (Defined)

1. **Academic Rhythm Layer**: Dynamic scoring that shifts priority based on the calendar (e.g., boosting study groups during finals, social mixers during welcome week).
2. **Semantic AI Vibe Matching**: Using LLMs (Gemini) to match the *description* of an event to a student's bio beyond simple keywords.
3. **Departmental Trending Heat**: Real-time boosts for events that are "blowing up" specifically within a student's major or department.
4. **Collaborative Filtering**: "Students similar to you also liked..." logic based on shared attendance history.
5. **Transparency Tooltips**: Explaining the "Why" (e.g., *"Matched because 4 friends are going"* or *"Matches your interest in Robotics"*).

---

## 👥 Phase 2: Social & Peer Dynamics

6. **Social Graph Density**: Boosting events where multiple *distinct* friend groups are intersecting.
7. **Freshman Integration Boost**: Stronger social-proof weighting for 1st-year students to help them build their first campus networks.
8. **Group RSVP Synergy**: Recommending events to entire friend groups simultaneously via shared notifications.
9. **Upperclassman Mentorship**: Suggesting events where seniors or alumni from the student's major are verified attendees or speakers.
10. **"Active Now" Proximity**: A massive boost for events starting within 15 minutes that are in the same building as the student's current class.

---

## 🎓 Phase 3: Academic & Career Intelligence

11. **Major-Adjacent Discovery**: Recommending events in majors that commonly overlap (e.g., CS students seeing Math/Physics events, Business students seeing Psych/Econ).
12. **Career Goal Alignment**: Matching events to "Aspirational Skills" (e.g., suggesting a design workshop to a Dev who wants to be a Product Manager).
13. **Professor Endorsements**: Boosting events that have been "starred" or shared by the student's current semester instructors.
14. **Portfolio Synergy**: Prioritizing events that offer certificates, badges, or "LinkedIn-ready" skills.
15. **Shadow-Interest Tracking**: Tracking "hover time" on event cards to refine interests even if the user doesn't click "Save."

---

## 🎮 Phase 4: Gamification & Behavioral Logic

16. **Recommendation Streaks**: Rewarding users for attending 3+ recommended events in a row with "Campus Guide" status.
17. **The "Wildcard" Recommendation**: One daily suggestion completely outside the user's profile to prevent "filter bubbles" and encourage new hobbies.
18. **FOMO Triggers**: Increasing boost for high-demand events that have "Only 5 seats left" or "Waitlist moving fast."
19. **Senior Bucket List**: For final-year students, prioritizing "Campus Traditions" they haven't checked off yet.
20. **Introvert/Extrovert Mode**: Detecting preference for small, high-value workshops vs. massive 100+ person parties.

---

## 🍕 Phase 5: Logistics & Campus Vibe

21. **"Freebie" Sentiment Analysis**: AI scanning for "Free Food," "Pizza," or "Company Swag" to boost for budget-conscious students.
22. **Weather-Adaptive Logic**: Automatically shifting recommendations to indoor venues during rain/snow and outdoor venues during sunny days.
23. **Visual Aesthetic Matching**: Analyzing event posters (via AI) to match the visual "vibe" the user typically engages with (e.g., Minimalist vs. Brutalist).
24. **Night-Owl vs. Early-Bird**: Matching event times to the student's historical peak activity hours in the app.
25. **Venue Familiarity**: Occasionally suggesting events in new, unexplored campus buildings to encourage campus exploration.

---

## 🧠 Phase 6: Next-Gen Integration

26. **Course-Linked Recommendations**: Integrating with the syllabus to suggest "Revision Parties" for specific upcoming exam dates.
27. **Club Affinity Linking**: If a user is in "Robotics," recommending events from the "AI Society" or "Mechanical Engineering Forum."
28. **Sentiment-Based Ranking**: Prioritizing events with positive "Vibe Reviews" from previous years or similar past events.
29. **Real-time Crowdsourcing**: Boosting events that have a high "Check-in" rate in the last 30 minutes.
30. **Dynamic User Personas**: Allowing users to toggle between "Academic Mode," "Social Mode," and "Career Mode" to instantly re-rank their feed.
